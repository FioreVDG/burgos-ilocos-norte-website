GLIMPSE OF BURGOS

The municipality of Burgos is significantly depicted and identified by its official seal.

The Shield

It emphasizes that Burgos is one of the municipalities of the province of Ilocos Norte and is under the guidance of the provincial government.

The Lighthouse

It stands for the governing body and the different government agencies stationed within the locality which oversees how the municipality is managed.

The Light

It stands for the different services rendered or offered to the populace.

The Mountain and Sea

They symbolize the geographical location of the municipality for reason that Burgos is a coastal town and surrounded by verdant hills and mountains.
